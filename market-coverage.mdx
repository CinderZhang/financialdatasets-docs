### 📊 Our Data
- <b>100%</b> coverage of publicly-traded companies in the United States
- <b>Active</b> and <b>delisted</b> stocks
- <b>30+ years</b> of historical fundamentals data
- <b>30,000+</b> tickers in our database

Our specialty is fundamentals data like income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements.  We proud ourselves on having a high quality fundamentals dataset that is standardized and easy to access. 

Want to see which tickers are available? Simply append `/tickers` to any endpoint:
```
GET https://api.financialdatasets.ai/prices/tickers/
GET https://api.financialdatasets.ai/financials/tickers/
```

Please visit the "Our APIs" section to learn how to access each of our endpoints.

### 🚧 Current limitations
- Non-US markets are not yet available
- Options, indices, or currencies are not yet available

We're continuously expanding our coverage and plan to add international markets and additional instrument types in the future.
